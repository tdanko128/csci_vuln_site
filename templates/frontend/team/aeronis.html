Aeronis

Aeronis is a Skyborn Wizard, a reclusive order of scholars who dwell in the floating city of Aetheris. Known for his mastery of ancient lore and elemental magic, Aeronis joins the group to decipher the prophecy of the Dawnbringers and unlock the true power of the artifacts.

Though aloof and sometimes cryptic, Aeronis’s deep knowledge proves invaluable. He uncovers the location of the Pillar of Eternity and helps shield the heroes from magical threats.

Aeronis’s commitment to preserving the balance of Etheral and his ability to unlock the secrets of the past make him a vital member of the group.
