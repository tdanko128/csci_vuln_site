Finnwise

Finnwise, a small and unassuming villager from Evergrove, is dismissed by many as unimportant. However, his sharp wit, resourcefulness, and unwavering loyalty make him the true hero of the Etheral Realms.

While Lioran carries the Emberstone, it is Finnwise’s cleverness that ensures its safe passage. In the final moments, as the group reaches the Pillar of Eternity, Finnwise performs a selfless act that activates the Emberstone, reigniting the Pillar and banishing the Shadowborn.

Though his courage goes unnoticed by history, Finnwise’s story is a reminder that even the smallest among us can change the fate of the world.
